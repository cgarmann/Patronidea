Patron flow

From teaser to Deal Room.

One primary action per screen: search, request, propose, accept, pay.

See Vault teaser before payment.

Choose Basic, Pro or Enterprise and pay via Stripe.

Browse curated ideas with search, filters and favorites.

Open the idea detail page. No price or view counts are shown.

Send request with a short message through the platform.

Wait for approval and notification.

Open Deal Room after approval.

Submit proposal: buy, license or partnership.

Pay through Stripe Connect after agreement.

Receive receipt and deal summary.

Deal Room
